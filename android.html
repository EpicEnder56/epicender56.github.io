Android

As you may know, I am not an apple fan. Instead of using iPhones and iOS, I use Motorola phones and android. I prefer android for multiple reasons, the first being the price. Most android phones are sold at a reasonable price compared to the price of their components. However, the iPhone XS is sold at the price of £800, with the materials only costing £129. Also, older android devices aren't slowed down on purpose over time, unlike apple devices. So these are the reasons of why I prefer android to iOS.
